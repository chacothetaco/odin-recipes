Butternut Squash Soup

Description

Looking for that seasonal fall fix? This recipe is a delicious mix of sweet and savory that will help bring the flavors of fall to your table.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 butternut squash - peeled, seeded, and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon allspice
  • salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Once melted, add in the onion, garlic, and thyme and cook for about 5 minutes, until the onion has softened.
  2. Add the squash and chicken stock. Bring to a simmer and cook until the squash is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Crumble the bouillon into the soup. Season with cumin, allspice, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat.
  4. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup to a smooth consistency. Alternatively, pour the soup into a blender and puree after a few quick pulses.
