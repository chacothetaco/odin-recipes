Korean Turkey Bowls

This recipe is perfect for nights when you want something tasty and healthy, but don't have the time to commit to an elaborate meal. Serve over brown rice or spiralized veggies to complete this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 teaspons toasted sesame oil
  • 0.5 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 green onions, chopped
  • 4 cups hot cooked brown rice (or spiralized veggies)
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat a large skiller over medium-high heat. Add ground turkey and cook, stirring and breaking up into smaller pieces until browned, 5-7 minutes. Drain excess juice/grease.
  2. Stir in garlic, ginger, and sesame oil and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. Stire in soy sauce, brown sugar, and red pepper. Cook until turkey absorbs some of the sauce, about 7 minutes. Add half of green onions.
  4. Serve over hot cooked rice; garnish with sesame seeds and remaining green onions.
