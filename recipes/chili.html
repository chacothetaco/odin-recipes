Chili

Description

This classic chili recipe will remind you of what mom used to make, but with a healthy twist. Swap out the ground beef with some ground tukey, throw in some veggies, beans, and a blend of chili seasonings and you have yourself a warm, hearty meal. Perfect for those cool fall and winter evenings!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 jalapenos, diced
  • 1.5 lbs ground turkey
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons cumin
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder or minced garlic
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons pepper
  • 1.5 cups beef broth
  • 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatos
  • 1 (16 oz) can black beans
  • 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in pot over medium-high heat for 2 minutes.
  2. Add onion and jalapenos and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the ground turkey to the pot, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon as it cooks. Cook for 6-7 minutes until the turkey has browned, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the chili powder, cumin, tomato paste, garlic, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined.
  5. Add the broth, diced tomatoes (w/ juice), drained beans, and tomato sauce. Stir well.
  6. Bring the liquid to a low boil, then reduce the heat to gently simmer the chili, uncovered, for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
